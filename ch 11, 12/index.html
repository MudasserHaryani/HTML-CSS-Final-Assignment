PIAIC

GOVERNOR SINDH IT INITIATIVE FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND COMPUTING

Governor Sindh IT Initiave for Artificial Intelligence and Computing (GIAIC) is a privately fnded not-for-profit education initiative started by Governor of Sindh, Kamran Khan Tessori, Sir Zia Khan to help promote education, research and busines opportunities in emerging tech fields like

  • Cloud Native Inteliggence
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Blockchain
  • Internet Of Things

Cloud Native computing

The Cloud Native Computing program prepares the students to program in open source technologies like, JavaLinux, Docker, Kubernetes, Javascript, Node.js, React and React Native while introducing the students to development of Microservies and software development techniques on cloud platform like AWS

Artificial Intelligence

The artificial Intelligence program of GIAIC takes the students through the foundation of the AI technology, via teaching programming in open source languages like Java, Python, Numpy, Pandas, Tensorflow 2.0 and flask, and teaching deploying practival AI solutions and use cases, and ending with real-life examples of modern day AI in practice.

Blockchain

The main emphasis of Blockchain program is to understand the blockcahins like Hyperledger Ethereum and Hyperledger (Fabric), and learn to program in languages such as solidity, JavaScript on framework like Node.Js. the program also introduces them to development of Distributed Apps (DApps), frameworks like Web3 and Truffle and an in-depth understanding of Blockchain-as-a-service (BaaS) and how to develop and offer an Initial Coin Offering (ICO) of a crypto-token.

Internet Of Things

The Internet of things program introduces the students to open source development technologies like Rust, Embedded Rust, WebAssembly, Hyper, Diesel, Google Assistant, and Kubernetes while making them efficient in developing Micro Services for loT Edge, IoT cloud, and coice computing for device control.
